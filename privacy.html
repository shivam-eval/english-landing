Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 April 2026

This policy describes how SpeakMitra English (“SpeakMitra English”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our WhatsApp companion for spoken English practice (the “Service”).

We work directly with individual learners across India. The Service is delivered through WhatsApp: you send a voice note, and we reply with simple feedback on grammar, vocabulary, fluency, clarity, and pronunciation.

Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Contact details — the WhatsApp phone number you message us from, and any name you choose to share with us.
  • Voice recordings — the audio you send while practising spoken English.
  • Text messages — replies you send during onboarding and practice sessions, such as picking a topic, choosing a feedback language, or requesting another attempt.
  • Profile information — your learning goal, comfort level, preferred feedback language, and similar preferences, if you choose to share them.

Information generated by the Service

  • Transcripts — written transcriptions of your voice recordings.
  • Feedback — spoken-English feedback covering grammar, vocabulary, fluency, clarity, and pronunciation, along with corrected examples and model pronunciation samples.
  • Usage data — timestamps, attempt counts, and progress signals that help us improve the Service.

Information from WhatsApp

We receive messages and message metadata through the WhatsApp Business Platform operated by Meta. Your use of WhatsApp is governed by Meta’s own Privacy Policy.

How we use information

  • To run the practice session: transcribe your audio, generate feedback, and reply on WhatsApp in your preferred language.
  • To improve the quality of feedback and pronunciation samples. We do not use identifiable user recordings to train third-party models.
  • To send service messages on WhatsApp, such as practice prompts, feedback, progress updates, and support replies related to your learning activity.
  • To respond to enquiries and provide support.
  • To comply with applicable law.

How we share information

We share information only as needed to operate the Service:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors that help us run the Service, including cloud hosting, speech recognition, text-to-speech, automated language analysis, and analytics. They process data only on our instructions and under contractual confidentiality.
  • WhatsApp / Meta — messages necessarily transit Meta’s WhatsApp Business Platform.
  • Legal compliance — if required by a valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of users or the public.

We do not sell personal information. We do not share your recordings or transcripts for advertising.

WhatsApp communication choices

If you message SpeakMitra English on WhatsApp, we may reply on WhatsApp with practice prompts, feedback, progress updates, and support messages related to the Service. You can ask us to stop sending messages by replying “stop” on WhatsApp at any time.

Data retention

Voice recordings, transcripts, and feedback are retained for as long as your account is active, plus a reasonable period thereafter to support your learning journey (typically up to 12 months from your last activity). Aggregated, de-identified analytics may be retained longer. On written request, we will delete identifiable practice data within 30 days, subject to any legal retention obligations.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest for stored recordings and transcripts, access controls, and audit logging. No system is perfectly secure; we will notify affected users and authorities of a data breach as required by applicable law.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, to withdraw consent, or to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Indian users may exercise rights available under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

To exercise any of these rights, message us on WhatsApp at +91 63614 05165. We will respond within 30 days.

Minors

The Service is intended for adults. If you are under 18, please use the Service only with the consent of a parent or guardian. Parents and guardians may contact us at any time to review or delete their child’s practice data.

International transfers

We are based in India. Some service providers we use may process data outside India. Where this happens, we put in place contractual safeguards consistent with applicable law to protect your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the most recent version. Material changes will be communicated to active users where reasonably possible.
