Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 April 2026

This policy describes how VoiceEval Technologies Pvt Ltd (“Voice Evaluation”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when students and coaching institutes use our WhatsApp-based IELTS Speaking practice companion (the “Service”).

We work primarily with IELTS coaching institutes in India. If you are a student practising through your institute, your institute is the data controller for your enrolment information; we act as a processor on their behalf for the practice activity itself.

Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Contact details — the WhatsApp phone number you message us from, and the name your institute enrols you under.
  • Voice recordings — the audio you send while practising Speaking tasks (Part 1, Part 2 cue cards, Part 3).
  • Text messages — replies you send during a session (e.g. selecting a question, requesting a re-attempt).
  • Profile information — your target band, preferred feedback language, and exam date, if you choose to share these.

Information generated by the Service

  • Transcripts — written transcriptions of your voice recordings.
  • Evaluations — band-style feedback, scores against IELTS-aligned rubrics, and model pronunciation references.
  • Usage data — timestamps, attempt counts, and progress metrics shown on the institute dashboard.

Information from WhatsApp

We receive messages and metadata through the WhatsApp Business Platform operated by Meta. Your use of WhatsApp is governed by Meta’s own Privacy Policy.

How we use information

  • To run the practice session: transcribe your audio, evaluate it against IELTS-aligned rubrics, and reply with feedback in your preferred language.
  • To show your institute a record of your attempts and progress on their dashboard.
  • To improve the quality of evaluation and pronunciation feedback. We do not use identifiable student recordings to train third-party models.
  • To respond to enquiries and provide support.
  • To comply with applicable law.

How we share information

We share information only as needed to operate the Service:

  • Your coaching institute — sees your attempts, transcripts, scores, and progress for the cohort you belong to.
  • Service providers — trusted vendors that help us run the Service, including cloud hosting, speech recognition, and large-language-model inference. They process data only on our instructions and under contractual confidentiality.
  • WhatsApp / Meta — messages necessarily transit Meta’s WhatsApp Business Platform.
  • Legal compliance — if required by a valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of users or the public.

We do not sell personal information. We do not share student recordings or transcripts for advertising.

Data retention

Voice recordings, transcripts, and evaluations are retained for the duration of the institute’s active engagement with us, plus a reasonable period thereafter to support the student’s study cycle (typically up to 12 months). Aggregated, de-identified analytics may be retained longer. On written request from an institute or student, we will delete identifiable practice data within 30 days, subject to legal retention obligations.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest for stored recordings and transcripts, access controls, and audit logging. No system is perfectly secure; we will notify affected users and authorities of a data breach as required by applicable law.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, to withdraw consent, or to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Indian users may exercise rights available under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

To exercise any of these rights, write to us at shivam@voiceeval.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Minors

Some IELTS candidates are below 18. We do not knowingly enrol minors without their institute’s confirmation that appropriate parental or guardian consent has been obtained. Parents and guardians may contact us at any time to review or delete their child’s practice data.

International transfers

We are based in India. Some service providers we use may process data outside India. Where this happens, we put in place contractual safeguards consistent with applicable law to protect your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the most recent version. Material changes will be communicated to enrolled institutes by email.
